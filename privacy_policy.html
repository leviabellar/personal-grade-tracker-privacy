Privacy Policy for Personal Grade Tracker

Last updated: August 16, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Personal Grade Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

Photos and Images

Our app may access your device's camera and photo library to allow you to:

  • Take photos to attach to your grade entries
  • Select existing images from your photo library
  • Capture images of assignments, tests, or other academic materials

Local Data Storage

The app stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Grade entries and academic records
  • Photos and images you attach to entries
  • App preferences and settings
  • User authentication data (if using cloud sync)

2. How We Use Your Information

All information collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Providing grade tracking and academic management functionality
  • Storing and organizing your academic data
  • Displaying attached images within the app
  • Syncing data across your devices (if cloud sync is enabled)

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: All your grade data and images are primarily stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to external servers unless you explicitly enable cloud synchronization.

Cloud Storage (Optional): If you choose to enable cloud sync features, your data may be stored securely using Firebase services, which are governed by Google's privacy policies.

Security: We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your academic data and images remain private and are not shared with:

  • Educational institutions
  • Other students or users
  • Marketing companies
  • Any unauthorized third parties

5. Third-Party Services

Our app may use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): For optional cloud sync and authentication
  • Google Mobile Ads: For displaying advertisements (if applicable)

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of information.

6. Permissions Explained

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Camera: To take photos for your grade entries
  • Photo Library Access: To select existing images from your device
  • Storage Access: To save and retrieve your grade data and images
  • Internet Access: For optional cloud sync and ads (if applicable)

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as the app is installed on your device. If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data will be removed unless you have enabled cloud backup.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and export your data through the app's export features
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app or using in-app deletion features
  • Disable cloud sync at any time
  • Revoke camera and storage permissions through your device settings

9. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users of all ages, including students under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children beyond what is necessary for the app's functionality. Parents and guardians can review and delete their child's data at any time.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes through app updates or notifications.
